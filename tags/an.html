The Aṅguttara Nikāya

Also available: As a Syllabus
Subscribe via: RSS

The numerical basket is the last of the four main nikāyas of the Sutta Piṭaka, containing practical and memorable teachings from the early sangha. The collection is roughly parallel to the Ekottara Āgama preserved in China.
