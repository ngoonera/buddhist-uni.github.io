The Saṃyutta Nikāya

Subscribe via: RSS

The third and thematic nikāya of the Sutta Piṭaka, containing thousands of short, advanced teachings. The collection is parallel to the Saṃyukta Āgama preserved by the northern schools of Buddhism.
