Ocean of Milk, Ocean of Blood

A Mongolian Monk in the Ruins of the Qing

By Matthew W. King

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.
Online

What is the “otherwise” of modernism in Mongolia and Inner-Asia?

A sketch of the life and works of Zawa Damdin: a prolific, Mongolian historian who lived through—and theorized—the destruction of his tradition.

Published in 2019 by The New Books Network.
