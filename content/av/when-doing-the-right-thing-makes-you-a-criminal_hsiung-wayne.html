When Doing the Right Thing Makes You a Criminal

By Wayne Hsiung and Ezra Klein

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.
Online

Set aside all the social norms we have, the expectations we have about who animals are or what is appropriate to do for animals and just ask: What would you do—what do you think the right thing to do is—if you saw an animal suffering?

A long and emotional interview with the founder of Direct Action Everywhere on why he cares so much about animal suffering and what drove him to risk so much fighting it.

About: Engaged Buddhism

Published in 2019 in Vox.
