Saṃyukta Āgama Parallels

By Charles Patton

Online spreadsheet

A spreadsheet showing the reconstructed “proper” order of the SA discourses, along with a table of their known parallels in the Pāli and Āgama Canons.

The table is based on Yinshun’s original work on the SA in Chinese. You can find a discussion about the table on SuttaCentral here.

About: The Saṃyukta Āgama

Published in 2019.
