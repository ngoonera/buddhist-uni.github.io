Reading Faithfully

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.
Online

If you are looking to dive into the suttas, there are many good pointers and valuable resources to be found on this blog.

A good place to start, is their How To Guide but I also love the posts on keeping a personal “medicine cabinet” and on using sutta checklists.

About: The Suttas The Early Buddhist Texts The Pāli Canon

Published in 2011.
