The Buddhist Wheel Symbol

By T. B. Karunaratne

Online pdf epub

The dhamma-cakka, the ever moving Wheel of Law, is the most prominent symbol of the Buddhists.

About: Forms of Buddhism Imagery in the EBTs Theravāda Buddhism

Featured in the course, " The Form of Buddhism."

Published in 1969 by The Buddhist Publication Society, Kandy as The Wheel number 137.
