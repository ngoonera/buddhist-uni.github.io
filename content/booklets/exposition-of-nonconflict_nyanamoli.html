The Exposition of Non-Conflict

MN 139

By Bhikkhu Ñāṇamoli Thera

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.
Online pdf epub

An important sutta on Right Speech, giving the Buddha’s famous injunction to “not insist on local language.”

About: Buddhist Ethics

Featured in the course, " Buddhist Ethics."

Published in 1979 by The Buddhist Publication Society, Kandy as The Wheel number 269.
