The Mirror of the Dhamma

A Manual of Buddhist Chanting and Devotional Texts

By Ven Nārada Mahāthera and Bhikkhu Kassapa

Online pdf

A collection of Buddhist devotional chants common in Sri Lanka.

About: Theravāda Buddhism

Published in 1963 (Laurence Khantipalo Mills ed.) by The Buddhist Publication Society, Kandy as The Wheel number 54.
