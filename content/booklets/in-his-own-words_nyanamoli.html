The Buddha’s Teachings in His Own Words

By Bhikkhu Ñāṇamoli Thera

Online pdf epub

A short anthology of texts from the Pali Canon framed by the Noble Eightfold Path. An excellent study guide.

About: The Buddhist Path The Pāli Canon The Early Buddhist Texts The Function of Buddhism

Published in 1999 by The Buddhist Publication Society, Kandy as The Wheel number 428.
