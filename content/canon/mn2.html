MN 2

Sabbāsava Sutta (All the Taints)

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.
Online pdf

Diverse problems demand a diverse range of responses. Rather than selling a “one size fits all” solution, in this sutta the Buddha outlines seven methods for dealing with the afflictions of life and in so doing gives us a comprehensive overview of Buddhist practices.

About: The Buddhist Path Buddhist Meditation Buddhist Ethics Buddhist Philosophy The Majjhima Nikāya The Pāli Canon

Translated by Bhikkhu Bodhi. Published in 2009.
