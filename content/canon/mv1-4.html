Mv 1–4

The Great Chapter of the Vinaya

📦 Archived
This work is only recommended for those with a special interest.
Online pdf epub

The opening four chapters of the Vinaya record the inspiring story of the Buddha’s enlightenment and of his first, eventful year of teaching.

About: The Vinaya

Translated by Bhikkhu Ānandajoti. Published in 2014.
