Ud 8.8

Visāka

Online pdf

For those who have one love, they have one suffering.
For those who love nothing, they have no sorrow.

About: Imagery in the EBTs Nibbāna

Featured in the course, " Imagery in the Early Buddhist Texts."

Translated by Bhikkhu Ānandajoti. Published in 2008.
