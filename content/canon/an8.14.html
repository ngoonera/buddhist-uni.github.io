AN 8.14

Wild Colts

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.
Online pdf

On the eight ways that people become defensive when admonished: a useful mirror for how we handle criticism. When was the last time you were “like a wild colt?”

About: Buddhist Ethics The Aṅguttara Nikāya Vinaya Studies Imagery in the EBTs

Featured in the course, " Buddhist Ethics."

Translated by Bhikkhu Bodhi. Published in 2012.
