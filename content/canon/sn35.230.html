SN 35.230

The Fisherman Simile

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.
Online

If a bhikkhu seeks delight in [the senses], welcomes them, and remains holding to them, he is called a bhikkhu who has swallowed Mara’s hook. He has met with calamity and disaster, and the Evil One can do with him as he wishes.

About: Imagery in the EBTs The Saṃyutta Nikāya Nibbāna

Featured in the course, " Imagery in the Early Buddhist Texts."

Translated by Bhikkhu Bodhi. Published in 2000.
