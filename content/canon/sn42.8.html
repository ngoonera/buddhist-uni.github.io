SN 42.8

A Horn Blower

Online pdf

The Buddha rejects the poorly phrased fatalism of a Jain follower and gives an alternative method for overcoming bad karma.

About: Buddhist Ethics The Saṃyutta Nikāya

Featured in the course, " Buddhist Ethics."

Translated by Bhante Sujato. Published in 2018.
