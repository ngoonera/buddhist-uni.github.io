MN 39

Mahā Assapura Sutta (The Greater Discourse at Assapura)

Online pdf

What are the qualities that make one a contemplative?

The Buddha gives an overview of the path from the perspective of ethics, from the establishment of shame all the way to the realization of the highest good: Nibbāna.

About: Buddhist Ethics The Majjhima Nikāya Monastic Buddhism Vinaya Studies The Buddhist Path

Featured in the course, " Buddhist Ethics."

Translated by Ajahn Geoff Ṭhānissaro. Published in 2008.
