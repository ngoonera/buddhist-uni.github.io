Bodhicaryāvatāra Chapter 3

Fully Adopting Bodhicitta

By Śāntideva

Online pdf

For all the beings ailing in the world,
Until their sickness has been healed,
May I become the doctor and the cure

A free translation of chapter 3 from the Bodhisattvacaryāvatāra on joyfully taking hold of “bodhicitta.”

About: Mahāyāna Buddhism

Published in 720.
