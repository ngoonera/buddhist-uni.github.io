Nibbāna

By Bhikkhu Bodhi

pdf pdf Origin

A condensed transcript of The Buddha’s Teaching As It Is lecture six, this short essay gives a definition and typology of nibbāna.

About: Nibbāna

Featured in the course, " Nibbāna: The Goal of Buddhist Practice."

Published in 1981.
