The Meaning of the Word Tathāgata According to the Pāli Commentaries

Text and Introduction

By Bhikkhu Bodhi

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.
pdf pdf pdf

In recognition of its pre-eminence among the Master’s epithets, the early Buddhist teachers and their successors have applied their wisdom and erudition to fathoming the multiple implications of this suggestive word.

About: The Buddha The Pāli Canon

Featured in the course, " The Buddha."

Published in 1978 in Pali Buddhist Review.
