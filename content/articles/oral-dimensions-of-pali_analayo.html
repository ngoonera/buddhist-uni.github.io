Oral Dimensions of Pāli Discourses

Pericopes, Other Mnemonic Techniques and the Oral Performance Context

By Bhikkhu Anālayo

pdf pdf

Examines the literary style of the Pāli Canon and explains how its textual features are a product of its performative context.

About: The Pāli Canon The Early Buddhist Texts The Suttas

Published in 2007 in The Canadian Journal of Buddhist Studies.
