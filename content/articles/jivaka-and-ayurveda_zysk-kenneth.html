Studies in Traditional Indian Medicine in the Pāli Canon

Jīvaka and Āyurveda

By Kenneth G. Zysk

Online pdf

The Pali account of the physician Jīvaka illustrates a well-established āyurvedic medical tradition and preserves at least one practice not found in classical āyurveda.

Comparing Jīvaka’s story to the old āyurvedic texts.

About: Characters in the EBTs Buddha's India

Published in 1982 in The Journal of the International Association of Buddhist Studies.
