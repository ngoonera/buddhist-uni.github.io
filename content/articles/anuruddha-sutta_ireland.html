Comments on the Anuruddha Sutta

By John D. Ireland

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.
pdf pdf Origin

By the ariya, the cessation of sakkaya is seen as happiness. This is the reverse of the outlook of the entire world!

About: Nibbāna

Featured in the course, " Nibbāna: The Goal of Buddhist Practice."

Published in 1996 in Buddhist Studies Review.
