Priming the Lamp of Dhamma

The Buddha’s Miracles in the Pāli Mahāvaṃsa

By Kristin Scheible

Online pdf

Much as a soaking in good oil will prime a lamp’s wick for the lighting, miracle stories prepare the audience for the cultivation of potent emotions and resultant ethical transformation.

About: Forms of Buddhism

Published in 2010 in The Journal of the International Association of Buddhist Studies.
