Illness as Metaphor

By Susan Sontag

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.
pdf pdf

the most truthful way of regarding illness — and the healthiest way of being ill — is one most puriﬁed of, most resistant to, metaphoric thinking

A classic and much-cited essay on the (mis)use of metaphors to describe disease.

Available online from the original publisher: Part 1, Part 2, and Part 3. Years later, Sontag also wrote in the NYRB, this time on the metaphors of AIDS in a compelling post-script later published alongside the original essay.

Published in 1978 in The New York Review of Books.
