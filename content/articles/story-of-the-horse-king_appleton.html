The Story of the Horse-King and the Merchant Siṃhala, in Buddhist Texts

By Naomi Appleton

Online pdf

Tracks one fable as it moved out of India and through the Buddhist world, giving us a glimpse into both the historical places Buddhism spread to and the process of mythic assimilation.

About: Forms of Buddhism Early Indian Buddhism

Featured in the course, " The Form of Buddhism."

Published in 2007 in Buddhist Studies Review.
