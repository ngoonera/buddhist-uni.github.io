The Suttas on Sakka in Āgama and Nikāya Literature

Some Remarks on the Attribution of the Shorter Chinese Saṃyukta Āgama

By Marcus Bingenheimer

Online pdf

An analysis of the possible sectarian affiliation of the BZA (T.100) based on its handling of Sakka, along with translations of said Sūtras.

About: The Saṃyukta Āgama

Published in 2008 in Buddhist Studies Review.
