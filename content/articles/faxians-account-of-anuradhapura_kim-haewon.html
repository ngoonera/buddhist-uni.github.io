Images and Monasteries in Faxian’s Account on Anurādhapura

By Haewon Kim

Online pdf

valuable material for the contemplation of the transit of ideas between South Asia and Korea

A Chinese monk visits medieval Sri Lanka and perhaps influences Korean sculpture, challenging our notions of nationalized Buddhisms.

About: Forms of Buddhism Pilgrimage

Featured in the course, " The Form of Buddhism."

Published in 2019 in The Hualin International Journal of Buddhist Studies.
