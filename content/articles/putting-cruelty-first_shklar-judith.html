Putting Cruelty First

By Judith Shklar

🥇 Featured
One of the best works in the library, "Putting Cruelty First" was featured in the March 2020 newsletter and is highly recommended for all.
Online pdf

In this essay, Judith Shklar (not a Buddhist) ponders the implications of placing cruelty first (as the Buddha did). She shows how this position stands at odds with both Christian piety and neoliberal apathy and carves out a more realistic humanism than either extreme.

About: The Function of Buddhism Buddhist Ethics Engaged Buddhism

Featured in the course, " The Function of Buddhism."

Published in 1982 in Dædalus.
