Stūpa, Sūtra, and Śarīra in China, c. 656–706 CE

By T. H. Barrett

🥇 Featured
One of the best works in the library, "Stūpa, Sūtra, and Śarīra in China, c. 656–706 CE" was featured in the November 2021 newsletter and is highly recommended for all.

what was the religious environment that encouraged the spread of the new technology of printing in late seventh century China?

The captivating story of how Empress Wu’s struggle for legitimacy led to the printing of the first mass-produced Buddhist text.

Published in 2001 in Buddhist Studies Review.
