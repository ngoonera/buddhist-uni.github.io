Crossing to the Farthest Shore

How Pāli Jātakas Launch the Buddhist Image of the Boat onto the Open Seas

By Sarah Shaw

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.
Online pdf

Buddhist literature offers us the only narratives from this period that feature to any great extent the nautical or maritime traveller as hero.

About: Early Indian Buddhism Imagery in the EBTs
