Far From the Madding Strife for Hollow Pleasures

Meditation and Liberation in the Śrāvakabhūmi

By Florin Deleanu

🥇 Featured
One of the best works in the library, "Far From the Madding Strife for Hollow Pleasures" was featured in the March 2021 newsletter and is highly recommended for all.
Online pdf

A very brief summary of the Śrāvakabhūmi: an ancient meditation manual preserved by the Yogācāra school.

For a more detailed, structural analysis, see “Some Remarks on the Textual History of the Śrāvakabhūmi” by the same author.

About: Early Indian Buddhism The Buddhist Path Early Sects

Published in 2012 in The Journal of the International College for Postgraduate Buddhist Studies.
