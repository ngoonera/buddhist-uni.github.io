The Oldest Known Pali Texts, 5–6th century

Results of the Cambridge Symposium on the Pyu Golden Pali Text from Śrī Kṣetra, 18–19 April 1995

By Janice Stargardt

pdf pdf

A description of some Pāli texts found inscribed on gold in an old Burmese stupa which demonstrate the care with which the Pāli tradition has been preserved even during the early medieval period.

Published in 1995 in The Journal of the Pali Text Society.
