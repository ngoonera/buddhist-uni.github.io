Chos sbyin gyi mdo

Bhikkhunī Dhammadinnā Proves Her Wisdom

By Bhikkhu Anālayo

pdf pdf

A translation of the Tibetan parallel to MN 44, showcasing the Arahant Bhikkhunī Dhammadinnā’s profound explanations of the Dhamma.

About: Characters in the EBTs Buddhist Philosophy The Buddhist Path Nibbāna

Published in 2011 in The Journal of Chinese Buddhist Studies.
