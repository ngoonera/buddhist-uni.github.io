Buddhism and Secular Subjectivities

Individualism and Fragmentation in the Mirror of Secularism

By David L. McMahan

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.
Online pdf

If the fragmenting forces of late modernity have shattered the illusion of a fixed self, anātman provides a way of rethinking subjectivity in its absence.

Published in 2021 in “Secularizing Buddhism” (Richard K. Payne ed.) by Shambhala Publications, Boulder, CO.
