Evolving Portrayals of Sāriputta and Moggallāna

Psychic Potency vis-à-vis Wisdom and Concentration

By Tse-fu Kuan

Online pdf

The Buddhist tradition has tended to associate Moggallāna with concentration or serenity, and Sāriputta with wisdom or insight, and to characterize the former figure along with his outstanding faculty as inferior to the latter.

About: Early Indian Buddhism Characters in the EBTs

Published in 2021 in “Illuminating the Dharma: Buddhist Studies in Honour of Venerable Professor KL Dhammajoti” (Toshiichi Endo ed.) by The Centre of Buddhist Studies at The University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong.
