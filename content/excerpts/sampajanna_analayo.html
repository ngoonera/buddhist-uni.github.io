Sampajañña (Clear Comprehension)

By Bhikkhu Anālayo

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.
pdf pdf

A concise definition of sampajāna explaining how it prepares the ground for formal meditation.

About: Buddhist Meditation The Buddhist Path

Published in 2006 in “Encyclopaedia of Buddhism” (W. G. Weeraratne ed.) by The Department of Buddhist Affairs, Sri Lanka.
