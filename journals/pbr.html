Pali Buddhist Review

Published by Pali Buddhist Union

Find the periodical online here.

Pali Buddhist Review was produced from 1976 to 1982 (to Vol. 6, no.2) by the Pali Buddhist Union, and was edited by Russell Webb. In 1983, it was superceded by Buddhist Studies Review.

The Meaning of the Word Tathāgata According to the Pāli Commentaries: Text and Introduction (1978) – Bhikkhu Bodhi

In recognition of its pre-eminence among the Master’s epithets, the early Buddhist teachers and their successors have applied their wisdom and erudition to fathoming the multiple implications of this suggestive word.
